Web Developer

I’m looking to work with, and support, local businesses that are looking to increase, update, or enhance, their presence on the web.

I think it's important to work with and support local businesses and start-ups as a website can provide great benefits, but isn't a cost that is top of the list of expenses.

By developing your own website you can create a bespoke tool that incorporates branding and marketing materials. It can provide additional information on the products and services you provide.

A website can also be linked to existing sites hosted through social media platforms, such as Facebook, Twitter or Instagram, to fully integrate your marketing and messaging.

I provide the following services:

  • Design and build bespoke websites
  • which can be:
  • - integrated with existing social media accounts
  • - contain responsive design elements - ie. websites that also work on smartphones and tablets
  • Review, update and refresh existing websites
  • Data analytics - tools that can help you understand your clients and potential customers

I would recommend that potential clients consider what styles, format and fonts they would like to use, this could be based on other marketing materials and websites.
Note - This is not essential.

Pricing would be dependent of complexity of requirements for the website and volume of content. This and payment methods can be discussed.
